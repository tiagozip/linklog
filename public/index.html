What is LinkLog?

LinkLog is a powerful URL shortener and analytics platform that provides detailed insights about link interactions. Track over dozens of data points per visitor with our advanced fingerprinting.

Unlike Grabify, LinkLog isn't sketchy, is significantly more transparent, powerful, and easy to use.

Is this legal to use?
Yep! We collect publicly available information that every website you go to can access. However, using this data to harm or doxx others is illegal.
